Work

HuffPost I’m a news editor with HuffPost. I edit reports and articles for verticals across the website, including breaking news, general assignment reports and long-form journalism. I work with our politics, trends and vertical reporters, as well as senior editors and the photo team, to make sure our coverage is accurate. I believe it’s important to both encourage writers to dive deeper into their reporting and to interrogate society's hidden assumptions and biases.

teleSUR English I worked as an editor for teleSUR's English-language office. I coached a team of diverse writers on how to report on Latin American news, with a special focus on progressive social movements. I edited reports and scripts from correspondents across Central America, South America and the Carribbean. As a commissioning op-ed editor, I helped to diversify our content, working with up-and-coming journalists and writers from a variety of backgrounds whose articles were widely shared by both Latino and Caribbean influencers in the U.S.
